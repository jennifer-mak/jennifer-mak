publication: '*Neuropsychological Rehabilitation*'
doi: 10.1080/713755496
abstract: This paper describes a new neglect test, where patients are asked to spread
  out 16 cubes as evenly as possible over a 75 x 100 cm board 'as if they were buns
  on a baking tray'. A consecutive series of 52 brain-damaged patients and 30 control
  subjects were examined with the new test as well as with several commonly used neglect
  tests. The baking tray task proved to be a quick and yet sensitive test, suitable
  for screening purposes and longitudinal studies.
